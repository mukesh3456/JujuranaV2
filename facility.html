EAT & DRINK

Restaurants & In-Room Dining

WINGS - TASTE THE WORLD

Our restaurant “Wings” draws inspiration from the grey, black, and pale brownish wings of the magnificent bird Jujurana.

It is a multi-cuisine all-day dining restaurant which is open to all and is set up in a 2000 sq ft area which is centrally air-conditioned. Opening up into a terrace with an unhindered view of the Kanga valley all up to the Pong Dam. A perfect blend of delectable cuisine and breathtaking scenery making it a haven for food and peace lovers; feast for all our senses. Our comprehensive menu is impeccable offering a wide variety of cuisines for varied palates. To enhance the experience, Wings occasionally organizes special live music and DJ nights creating an otherworldly atmosphere for both our out-of-town guests and the locals of Dharamshala.

HP68 AT 4455

Vibrant yet casual ambience opening into the terrace overlooking the an amazing panoramic view of the valley & Dhauladhars, sitting at an altitude of 4455 FT with a glass in your hand; Oh! what a feeling! Open from 11am till 10pm and offering a variety of beverages along with a finger snack one can spend an entire day admiring the change in the scenery and colors of nature in time lapse. The menu is delightful, defies stereotypes, and boldly brings quirky simple dishes and comfort food to the table. It’s leisure, it’s peculiar; yet, it’s unabashedly Himachali. Hp 68 AT 4455 offers anything but the usual.

JACU CAFE

For the ones who enjoy a hot or cold brew, with a view… we have; Jacu. A coffee shop serving the best coffees, sandwiches and snacks for you to fill up on before the day starts or end your day with. Not just that, we also have an elaborate assortment of mouthwatering pastries and cakes to accompany your coffee.

Jacu - the bird knows their coffee too well and is known to select some of the finest plants for coffee beans thus making an ideal name for our coffee shop. We have you covered!

Sheesha Lounge

Step into Sheesha Lounge, where every puff is a journey of flavor and relaxation. Our cozy space invites you to unwind with premium hookah blends and delectable bites. It's not just a lounge, it's your retreat for laid-back moments and exceptional hookah experiences. Join us for a flavorful escape.
