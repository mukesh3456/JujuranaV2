EAT & DRINK

Restaurants & In-Room Dining

WINGS - TASTE THE WORLD

Our restaurant, Wings, draws inspiration from the grey, black, and pale brownish wings of the magnificent bird, Jujurana. It is a multi-cuisine all-day dining restaurant, set up in a 2000 sq ft area which is centrally air-conditioned. Opening up to a terrace, it offers an unobstructed view of the Kanga valley extending all the way to the Pong Dam. A perfect blend of delectable cuisine and breathtaking scenery. Because isn't that what vacations are all about? Our comprehensive menu is impeccable, but we highly recommend indulging in our Wings specialty: chicken wings prepared in various mouthwatering ways, paired perfectly with the drink of your choice. To enhance the experience, Wings hosts special live music events on weekends, creating an otherworldly atmosphere for both our out-of-town guests and the locals of Dharamshala.

HP68 AT 4455

Our casual bar is situated at a height of 4455 feet above the sea-label. Open from 11am till 11pm. A Casual seating style opens into the terrace overlooking the valley. Offering a variety of cocktails, mocktails, and liquor along with a finger snack menu curated for the tipsy. It’s leisure, it’s peculiar; yet, it’s unabashedly Himachali. Hp 68 – offers anything but the usual. The menu is delightful, defies stereotypes, and boldly brings quirky simple dishes and comfort food to the table. All are backed by inspired cocktails and a solid bar

JACU CAFE

Presenting Jacu, our coffee and pastry haven designed for those who relish a scenic sip, whether hot or cold. Delight in the finest coffees, sandwiches, and snacks to jumpstart or unwind your day. But that's not all, we also have an elaborate assortment of mouth watering pastries and cakes to accompany your coffee. We have you covered!

Sheesha Lounge

Step into Sheesha Lounge, where every puff is a journey of flavor and relaxation. Our cozy space invites you to unwind with premium hookah blends and delectable bites. It's not just a lounge, it's your retreat for laid-back moments and exceptional hookah experiences. Join us for a flavorful escape.
