About Jujurana

Jujurana means “King of birds". It's a magnificent bird which was awarded a status of the State Bird of Himachal Pradesh in 2007. Jujurana is an endangered species and is considered the rarest of all living pheasants; captivating your attention with its beauty and crimson neckline. The world population of Jujurana is estimated at fewer than 5,000 individuals, including a captive breeding population in Himachal Pradesh which numbered fewer than ten pairs in 2012.

Our logo “Jujurana" is inspired by the beauty and the vivid blue and red colors of the Jujurana bird. The script font has been selected for this logo to symbolize the vast openness of the vicinity. It stands for spontaneity, the free flowing creativity and the contemporary luxury that this resort has to offer.

Jujurana Resort & Spa, Dharamshala, is one of the finest family resorts in the area and a dream destination for anyone who always wanted a retreat in the mountains. The resort has a breathtaking view of the snow clad Dhauladhar Mountains along with thick pine woods and bird's eye view of the Kangra valley.

AMENITIES & SERVICES

Jujurana is designed in contemporary Himachali style to blend seamlessly with the natural surroundings and offering high-class amenities. There are 21 rooms in 5 cottage blocks. Room size varies between 400 sq. ft to 900 sq. ft. with 5 Duplex suites and all our rooms have either a private garden or a balcony to enjoy a perfect view.

The Jujurana Resort & Spa is not just a place to stay, but a sanctuary of tranquility and rejuvenation for those seeking a respite from the hustle and bustle of city life. It is situated next to the Helipad in Rakkar offering a surreal experience all around. The resort houses.
