About Jujurana

“Jujurana”; our logo, is inspired by the vivid blue and red colours of the Jujurana bird. The script font has been selected for this logo to symbolize the vast openness of the vicinity. It stands for spontaneity, the free flowing creativity, and the contemporary luxury that this resort has to offer.

Jujurana means “King of birds“. It was accorded the status of state bird of Himachal Pradesh in 2007.

Jujurana Resort & Spa, Dharamshala is one of the finest family resorts in the area, and a dream destination for anyone who always wanted a retreat in the mountains!

This is what makes us one of the most ideal resorts up here in the mountains;An ABSOLUTELY surreal escapade for family getaways, staycations, workcations and destination weddings.

AMENITIES & SERVICES

Seamless blend of Himachali & Tibetan architecture, our resort is situated next to the Helipad in Rakkar. Picturesque green lawns and an alluring view of Dharamshala flank the property; which houses a, multi-cuisine restaurant, a contemporary bar, coffee shop, cinema hall, Hookah Bar, a conference room, wellness zone comprising of a spa, a pristine heated pool, Yoga & meditation area and a kids’ club to keep you busy all day long. A spacious lounge overlooking the vast valley to host private events.

Our architects and designers constantly and carefully monitor the environment, they accept and develop changes, research fashion and architectural, as well as sociological, changes and transform them into unique design.
